Sequencer

This is the sequencer grid where you are able to collaboratively layer samples onto the columns.

Drag from the samples bank onto the sequencer, hit the cross on the individual sample to delete and hit the play button in the top right or [spacebar] to start playing each column, one after the other to see what nice combinations you can come up with!
